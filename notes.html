Lecture Notes

During my time at university, I wrote a few sets of lecture notes/guides that are still being used today.

To typeset my notes, I use a language called LaTeX. Here is a link to a fantastic introductory tutorial written by cnguyen-uk.

Below is a brief history of my work:

University of Warwick

2015 - Vector Analysis (Revision Guide)

I typeset this revision guide for MA231 in August 2016 and it was distributed by the Warwick Maths Society in the 2016-2017 academic year.

2015 - Stochastic Processes

I typeset ST202 in September 2019 for personal use.

King's College London

2018 - MSc Topology

I decided to create my own set of lecture notes as I took the course. These notes have since been used by Prof. Igor Wigman during both the 2017-18 and 2018-19 academic years. At least 100 students have made use of them so far.
